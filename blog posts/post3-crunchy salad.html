Crunchy Chicken Salad


  • Cooking time: 20 mins
  • Prep time: 10 mins
  • Total time: 30 mins
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients


  • 2 cups sliced chicken (baked)
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts/almonds
  • 1 cups shredded red cabbage or lettuce
  • 1/2 cup carrots (julienne)
  • 1/2 cup celery (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup green/red bell pepper (julienne)
  • 1/4 cup of any of your choice of green leafy vegetable

    • Dressing


  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground mustard
  • 1/4 cup yoghurt, plain
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Preparation


  • In a bowl toss chicken, peanuts/almonds, and all the vegetables.
  • Mix all the dressing ingredients in a jar or bowl.
  • Pour the dressing over the tossed salad and toss until perfectly coated.

Helpful tips


  • Drain all the water remaining in the vegetables thoroughly before adding the dressing to ensure a nice coating of dressing and a better taste
  • Add some roasted pumpkin seeds for more crunchiness in your salad.
  • Always try for boiled or baked chicken rather than fried.
  • If you are in a weight loss diet, prefer not adding honey and yoghurt in your salad.
